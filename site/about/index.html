About

Online workshop open for PhD students, postdocs, researchers and other employees within Swedish academia. This workshop builds on our previous ChIP-seq data analysis workshop to introduce the best practice bioinformatics methods for processing, analyses and integration of epigenomics data. The teaching includes lectures, programming tutorials and interactive group sessions.
